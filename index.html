PR Director

The Stuco PR director is charged with all manner of, well, public relations tasks; duties ranging from poster design to social media management to coordination with clubs around the school. My goal isn't to revolutionize the school or anything, it's simply to do my job well: get the message across, whether that means a poster advertising an event or perhaps one of those annoying mass emails you get from your Stuco senators.
